# Privacy Policy — Advance Task Manager

Last updated: April 2026

## Data We Collect
Advance Task Manager collects anonymous usage data to help us 
improve the app. This includes events such as app opens, 
view switches, and task actions.

## What We Do NOT Collect
- Your name
- Your email address
- Any task content (titles, descriptions, tags)
- Any personally identifiable information

## Third-Party Services
We use Firebase Analytics (Google) to process anonymous usage data.
You can read Google's privacy policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy
